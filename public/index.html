Welcome to the Rule of 3 Calculator!

Solve proportional problems quickly. Just enter your known values, and we'll do the rest!

How it works:

  1. Input values: Enter two known quantities and their values.
  2. Find the unknown: Enter the single known value for the second quantity.
  3. Calculate: Click "Calculate" for the result.

Example:

2 apples cost $4, how much for 5 apples?
Input: 2, 4, 5
Result: $10
